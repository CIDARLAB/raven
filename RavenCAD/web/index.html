Why RavenCAD?

Here's a few ways RavenCAD will help you work faster

Save Time and Money

RavenCAD uses a sophisticated dynamic programming algorithm to design assemblies that are quicker and cheaper. RavenCAD produces SBOL compliant images that visualize your assembly plan as well as human readable instructions.

Assembly Methods

Design assemblies using popular and modern DNA assembly techniques such as MoClo and Gibson. RavenCAD's algorithms are broadly applicable and they will work with nearly all assembly techniques.

Built using Eugene and Clotho

RavenCAD leverages the Eugene design language to generate anywhere from dozens to thousands of genetic device designs. There's no better place for storing these designs than a Clotho data base.
