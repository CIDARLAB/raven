Support

Please send all questions and comments to ravencadhelp@gmail.com

Our dedicated support team is composed of software developers that have contributed to RavenCAD. We will address your questions and concerns in under 24 hours.

In your email, please follow these guidelines for email communication:

  • Indicate your name and professional affiliation
  • If you are a commercial partner of [INSERT COMPANY NAME] please contact your dedicated [INSERT COMPANY NAME] representative
  • If you are interested in forming an commercial or research relationship with [INSERT COMPANY NAME], please contact Mike directly
  • If your email is about a bug or problem in RavenCAD:
    • Please use a short description of the problem as the message header
    • Include the stack trace error message if applicable

We appreciate all forms of feedback.
